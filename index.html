01

Information We Collect

When you register or use OBI Chat, we collect only what's necessary to provide our service:

  • Full Name

02

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve OBI Chat:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Allow you to create and join private chat rooms

03

Data Visibility

Your personal information is never publicly visible. Access is strictly limited to:

We treat your privacy as a core feature, not an afterthought. Your name, email, and phone number are never displayed to other users outside your room.

04

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties, advertisers, or external organizations — ever.

05

Data Security

We take reasonable and industry-standard measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All data is stored securely and access is strictly controlled.

06

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

07

Children's Privacy

OBI Chat is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, it will be deleted promptly.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of OBI Chat after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
